4v4 PASS Time

What is 4v4 PASS Time?

4v4 PASS Time is a gamemode based off of the official PASS Time gamemode, but altered to allow for more competitive and fast-paced gameplay. 4v4 PASS Time focuses on mechanical skill (rocket jumps, airshots, trimps, surfs, etc) as well as solid teamwork and communication. Anyone is welcome to join and learn. Pugs will be balanced based on your skill level so you do not need to worry about dragging your team down if you are new. Like other competitive modes, we feature class limits: in our case, 3 soldiers, 1 demoknight, and 1 medic.

What basic information should I know?



The whitelist is on whitelist.tf. Only soldier, demoknight, and medic are allowed. Banned classes either slow down the game significantly or are outright overpowered. By testing the gamemode for over 5 years, the community decided that this whitelist was best.

How do I play?

You can play by joining our Discord. Our server frequently runs pugs (short for pick up games). By participating in pugs, you can show your skill and climb up the ranks from Passtime Newcomer to Passtime Grandmaster. Additionally, RGL and our community Discord collaborate to run passtime cups where you can test your skill and compete for keys.
